Refund & Cancellation Policy

This Refund and Cancellation Policy applies to all purchases made through LegendSpeak.net.

1. One-Time Payments

All purchases on LegendSpeak.net are one-time payments. There are no recurring subscriptions or automatic billing cycles.

2. Access Periods

Some offerings provide access for a fixed duration (e.g., 30 days). Once this period ends, access will automatically expire. No cancellation is required.

3. Lifetime Access

Lifetime access products grant indefinite access to the service. No cancellation is required for lifetime purchases.

4. Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be considered on a case-by-case basis under reasonable circumstances, including but not limited to:

• Unauthorized or fraudulent transactions
• Accidental duplicate purchases
• Technical issues preventing access to the service
• Service not delivered as described

5. Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will generally not be issued in the following situations:

• Change of mind after purchase
• Failure to use the service
• Dissatisfaction not related to a technical issue

6. Requesting a Refund

To request a refund, please contact our support team through the contact page:

Contact Support

Please include your email address, transaction details, and a clear explanation of your request.

7. Review Process

All refund requests are reviewed individually. Approved refunds will be processed back to the original payment method within a reasonable timeframe.
